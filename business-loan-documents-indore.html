Common documents

Business loan documents in Indore can change depending on the lender, business vintage, turnover, and loan type. Still, most MSME and working capital enquiries start with identity proof, business proof, banking history, and income or turnover evidence.

  • PAN, Aadhaar, business address proof.
  • Bank statement and business vintage proof.
  • GST/ITR details where applicable.
  • Sales, turnover, and loan purpose details.
