Privacy Policy

How RupeBazaar.in handles your enquiry data.

We collect only the details needed to understand your loan requirement and arrange a consultation callback.

Information we collect

When you submit a form, we may collect your name, phone number, email, city, loan type, loan amount, income, CIBIL score range, message, and page source.

How we use it

Your details are used to respond to your enquiry, understand eligibility, share callback support, and maintain internal lead records. We do not sell your personal information.

Sharing with partners

If you ask for loan assistance, your details may be shared with relevant bank, NBFC, DSA, or financial service partners only for loan consultation and application support.

Data safety

We use reasonable safeguards for enquiry data, but users should avoid sharing sensitive OTP, card PIN, full account passwords, or net-banking credentials.
