EMI kam karne ke practical ways

Home loan EMI mainly three things se decide hoti hai: loan amount, interest rate, and tenure. Agar EMI high lag rahi hai to tenure badhane se monthly EMI kam ho sakti hai, lekin total interest badh sakta hai. Isliye EMI calculator se multiple scenarios compare karna important hai.

Balance transfer tab useful ho sakta hai jab existing interest rate market se kaafi zyada ho aur transfer cost justify ho. Prepayment principal reduce karta hai, jisse EMI ya tenure dono me se ek impact ho sakta hai.

  • Higher down payment se loan amount kam hota hai.
  • Longer tenure EMI kam karta hai, interest badha sakta hai.
  • Balance transfer lower rate la sakta hai if total cost works.
  • Part-prepayment principal reduce karta hai.

FAQ

Home loan EMI questions

Long tenure lena sahi hai?

Long tenure EMI kam kar sakta hai, but total interest badh sakta hai. EMI comfort and total cost dono compare karein.

Balance transfer kab useful hota hai?

Jab existing rate high ho and transfer charges ke baad bhi saving meaningful ho.

Prepayment EMI kam karta hai?

Prepayment principal reduce karta hai. Lender rules ke hisab se EMI ya tenure reduce ho sakta hai.

EMI calculator exact hai?

Calculator estimate deta hai. Final EMI actual lender terms and charges ke baad confirm hoti hai.
