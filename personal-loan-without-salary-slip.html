What to know about Personal Loan Without Salary Slip

If salary slip is not available, some profiles may still be reviewed using bank statement, salary credit, ITR, employer certificate, or alternate income proof.

  • Bank statement with regular salary credit becomes very important.
  • Employer certificate or appointment letter may help in some cases.
  • Cash salary profiles are harder and may need stronger banking or alternate route.
  • Self-employed users should keep ITR and business banking ready.

Best way to proceed

Do not apply randomly everywhere. First compare eligibility, document readiness, CIBIL impact, fees, and repayment comfort. Then submit a guided enquiry only when you are ready.
