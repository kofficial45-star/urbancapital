Eligibility factors

Personal loan eligibility in Indore usually depends on repayment capacity first. A lender checks how much stable income you have, how much EMI is already running, and whether the new EMI looks comfortable after monthly expenses. RupeBazaar.in uses this page to collect the basic details before a manual callback.

  • Stable monthly income and job/business continuity.
  • Low existing EMI burden compared to income.
  • Clean repayment history and healthy credit behavior.
  • Aadhaar, PAN, bank statement, salary slip or income proof.

Personal loan eligibility FAQ

Does income guarantee approval?

No. Income helps, but credit profile, documents, existing EMI, and lender policy also matter.

Can self-employed users apply?

Yes. Business income, bank statement, and business stability are reviewed during callback.
