Document Guide

Personal loan documents checklist for India

Before applying, keep KYC, income proof, bank statement, CIBIL range, and existing EMI details ready. A clean document set makes callback and lender review smoother.

KYCIncome ProofBank StatementCIBIL Range

Basic documents for salaried borrowers

  • PAN card, Aadhaar card, mobile number, and email.
  • Last 3-6 month salary slips or salary certificate.
  • Last 3-6 month bank statement showing salary credits.
  • Current address proof if Aadhaar address is different.
  • Existing loan/credit card EMI details where applicable.

For self-employed borrowers

Self-employed profiles may need business proof, ITR, GST, bank statement, shop/office proof, and turnover details depending on partner/lender policy.
