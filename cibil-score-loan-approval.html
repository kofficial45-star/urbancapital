CIBIL score loan approval me kyu important hai?

CIBIL score repayment behavior ka signal hota hai, but approval sirf score se nahi, income and documents se bhi decide hota hai.

CIBILRepaymentEligibilityLoan Approval

750+

Generally stronger profile signal, but lender still checks income, EMI burden, and documents.

650-749

Possible options depend on lender, salary/business profile, and existing EMI load.

Below 650

Options can be limited. Secured loan, co-applicant, lower amount, or improved repayment history may help.
