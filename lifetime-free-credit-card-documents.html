What to know about Lifetime Free Credit Card Documents

Lifetime-free credit cards can be useful, but users should still check documents, eligibility, network, reward caps, and hidden conditions before applying.

  • Keep PAN, address proof, income proof, and mobile/email ready.
  • Check whether card is truly lifetime-free or only first-year-free.
  • Review reward caps, excluded categories, and annual fee waiver conditions.
  • Secured/FD-backed cards may ask for deposit or funding.

Best way to proceed

Do not apply randomly everywhere. First compare eligibility, document readiness, CIBIL impact, fees, and repayment comfort. Then submit a guided enquiry only when you are ready.
