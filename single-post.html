5 Smart Loan Strategies for 2025

April 26, 2025 Advisory Team 6 min read

In an era of shifting interest rates, geopolitical uncertainty, and rapid technological disruption, knowing where to put your money in 2025 has never been more important. Here are five proven strategies our advisors recommend.

1. Diversify Across Asset Classes

The cornerstone of smart loaning remains diversification. Spread your loans across equities, bonds, real estate, and alternative assets. In 2025, with AI-driven loans creating new sector volatility, a well-diversified loan acts as a natural shock absorber.

2. Prioritise Inflation-Resistant Assets

With inflation remaining a key concern, assets like Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS), commodities, and real estate can provide a natural hedge. Consider allocating a portion of your loan to these instruments to preserve purchasing power.

4. Focus on Quality Over Speculation

In uncertain times, companies with strong balance sheets, consistent earnings, and proven management teams tend to outperform speculative plays. Look for businesses with durable competitive advantages and pricing power.

5. Maximise Tax-Advantaged Accounts

Before loaning in a taxable account, ensure you're fully utilising 401(k)s, IRAs, and HSAs. The tax savings from these vehicles can significantly boost your long-term returns.
