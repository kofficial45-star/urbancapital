Step-by-step process

Personal loan ke liye pehla step eligibility samajhna hai. Bank ya NBFC usually monthly income, job stability, repayment history, and existing EMI burden check karta hai. Agar aap salaried hain to salary slip aur bank statement important hote hain. Self-employed users ke liye business banking, cash flow, and income proof important ho sakte hain.

Loan amount choose karte waqt sirf maximum approval mat dekho. EMI comfortable honi chahiye, warna approval ke baad repayment pressure badh sakta hai. Isliye pehle EMI calculator use karo, phir eligibility form submit karo.

Documents ready rakho

  • PAN, Aadhaar, mobile number and email.
  • Salary slip or income proof.
  • Last 3-6 month bank statement.
  • Existing EMI and loan details, if any.
