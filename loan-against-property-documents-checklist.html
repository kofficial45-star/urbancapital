What to know about Loan Against Property Documents Checklist

Loan against property needs stronger document preparation because both income and property papers are checked before sanction.

  • Keep ownership papers, title chain, property tax receipt, approved map, and address proof ready.
  • Income documents and bank statement are still important even though the loan is secured.
  • Legal and valuation checks can take time.
  • Never mortgage property without understanding EMI risk and total repayment.

Best way to proceed

Do not apply randomly everywhere. First compare eligibility, document readiness, CIBIL impact, fees, and repayment comfort. Then submit a guided enquiry only when you are ready.
