LAP me property collateral hoti hai. Isliye property title, valuation, income, repayment capacity, and legal checks bahut important hote hain.

LAP ke main checks

  • Property ownership and title clarity.
  • Property type, location, valuation, and age.
  • Applicant income, business stability, and bank statement.
  • Existing EMI, CIBIL behavior, and repayment comfort.

LAP ka tenure personal loan se longer ho sakta hai, but property pledged hoti hai. EMI default serious risk create kar sakta hai, isliye borrowing amount carefully choose karein.
