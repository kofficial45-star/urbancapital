Eligibility estimate ke main factors

Calculator exact approval guarantee nahi hota. Ye repayment comfort ka estimate deta hai. Lender final sanction ke liye KYC, credit bureau, income proof, bank statement, and internal policy check karta hai.

  • Net monthly income and employment stability.
  • Existing EMI and credit card obligation.
  • CIBIL score range and recent repayment behavior.
  • Loan amount, tenure, and approximate EMI comfort.
