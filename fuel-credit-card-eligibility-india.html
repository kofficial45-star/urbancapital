What to know about Fuel Credit Card Eligibility

Fuel credit cards are useful when monthly fuel spends are regular, but eligibility, surcharge waiver, reward caps, and eligible fuel partners must be checked carefully.

  • Check whether card benefits apply on IOCL, HPCL, BPCL, or selected partners only.
  • Fuel surcharge waiver usually has minimum/maximum transaction limits.
  • Rewards are often capped monthly or yearly.
  • Compare annual fee against actual fuel savings.

Best way to proceed

Do not apply randomly everywhere. First compare eligibility, document readiness, CIBIL impact, fees, and repayment comfort. Then submit a guided enquiry only when you are ready.
