Common Questions

Before you submit a loan enquiry

These answers help users understand the process before speaking with the team.

RupeBazaar.in loan deta hai kya?

RupeBazaar.in loan guidance, eligibility enquiry, EMI planning, and callback support provide karta hai. Final sanction bank/NBFC/lender policy se hota hai.

Eligibility check free hai?

Initial enquiry and callback flow free hai. Agar kisi bank, NBFC, or partner ka processing fee/applicable charge hai, wo product and approval terms ke hisab se hota hai.

CIBIL score low ho to loan milega?

Low score me options limited ho sakte hain. Income, collateral, banking history, co-applicant, and lender policy ke hisab se route discuss hota hai.

EMI calculator exact hota hai?

Calculator estimate deta hai. Final EMI actual rate, tenure, processing fees, insurance, GST, and approval terms ke baad confirm hoti hai.

Lead submit ke baad kya hota hai?

Details Google Sheet and email alert me save hoti hain. Team working hours me follow-up karke requirement, eligibility, and documents discuss karti hai.

Approval guaranteed hai?

No. RupeBazaar.in guidance and callback support deta hai. Final approval lender ke credit, income, document, and risk checks par depend karta hai.

Common rejection reasons

  • Low income compared to requested EMI.
  • High existing loan/credit card burden.
  • Missing or mismatched documents.
  • Low CIBIL score or recent repayment delays.

Charges to check

  • Processing fee and GST.
  • Insurance or optional bundled products.
  • Foreclosure/prepayment rules.
  • Late payment and bounce charges.

Improve approval chances

  • Keep documents and bank statements ready.
  • Choose EMI that matches income comfort.
  • Avoid multiple random applications.
  • Share correct income and existing EMI details.
