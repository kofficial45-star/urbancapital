What to know about RuPay Credit Card for UPI Payments

RuPay credit cards can be linked to UPI on supported apps and merchants, making them useful for everyday payments where card-on-UPI is accepted.

  • UPI linking works only for eligible RuPay credit cards and supported apps.
  • Not every UPI merchant may accept credit-card UPI payments.
  • Rewards and cashback can have merchant/category restrictions.
  • Pay full statement dues on time to avoid interest and late fees.

Best way to proceed

Do not apply randomly everywhere. First compare eligibility, document readiness, CIBIL impact, fees, and repayment comfort. Then submit a guided enquiry only when you are ready.
