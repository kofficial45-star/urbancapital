Loan balance transfer kab useful hota hai?

Balance transfer tab consider karein jab current loan rate high ho, repayment track clean ho, remaining tenure enough ho, and total savings processing fees se better ho.

Transfer se pehle kya compare karein?

  • Current interest rate vs new indicative rate.
  • Remaining principal and remaining tenure.
  • Processing fee, foreclosure/prepayment terms, insurance, and other charges.
  • Top-up requirement, if extra funds are needed.

Home loan balance transfer me long tenure hone par savings meaningful ho sakti hai. Personal loan transfer me total cost carefully compare karna zaroori hai.

Transfer worth hai ya nahi?

Current EMI, outstanding amount, rate, tenure, city, and CIBIL range submit karo.
