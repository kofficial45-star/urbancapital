Checklist for business loan

Business loan me lender business stability and repayment capacity dekhte hain. Isliye personal KYC ke saath business proof, bank statement, GST/ITR where applicable, turnover evidence, and loan purpose clear hona chahiye. Agar business new hai, route limited ho sakta hai; agar banking strong hai, chances better ho sakte hain.

  • PAN, Aadhaar, mobile, email.
  • Business address proof, shop act, GST, Udyam, or registration if available.
  • Last 6-12 month bank statement and turnover details.
  • GST returns, ITR, invoices, or sales records where applicable.

Common rejection reasons

Weak banking, frequent cheque bounce, low turnover, missing GST/ITR, high existing EMI, or unclear business purpose can reduce options.

Charges to check

Processing fee, GST, documentation charge, insurance, foreclosure rules, and renewal charges where applicable.

FAQ

Business loan document questions

GST nahi hai to business loan milega?

Possible routes depend on lender policy, business type, banking, turnover, and loan amount. GST helpful hota hai but every case different hota hai.

ITR mandatory hai?

Higher amount and formal business loans me ITR helpful hota hai. Some routes may review bank statement and business proof.

New business ko loan mil sakta hai?

New business me options limited ho sakte hain. Existing banking, collateral, co-applicant, or smaller ticket size help kar sakta hai.

Business loan approval guaranteed hai?

No. RupeBazaar.in guidance and enquiry support deta hai. Final approval lender policy par depend karta hai.
