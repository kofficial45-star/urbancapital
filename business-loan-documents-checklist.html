Checklist for business loan

Business loan me lender business stability and repayment capacity dekhte hain. Isliye personal KYC ke saath business proof, bank statement, GST/ITR where applicable, and loan purpose clear hona chahiye. Agar business new hai, to route limited ho sakta hai; agar banking strong hai, to chances better ho sakte hain.

  • PAN, Aadhaar, mobile, email.
  • Business address proof or shop establishment proof.
  • Bank statement and turnover details.
  • GST, ITR, invoices, or registration if available.
